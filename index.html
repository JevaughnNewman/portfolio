Data Analyst

i’m a data analyst with nearly six years of experience in the insurance industry, specializing in accident benefits and complex claims. I transform complex claims data into actionable insights using SQL, Power BI, Excel, Tableau, and Python, delivering dashboards and analytics solutions that reduce costs, improve workflows, and support strategic decision-making.

I design scalable, interactive dashboards to monitor trends, claims leakage, and reserve management, enabling faster, data-driven decisions in highly regulated environments. As a mentor to junior adjusters, I promote team development and clear collaboration across legal, finance, and operations.

Certified as a Microsoft Power BI Data Analyst Associate, I combine in-depth industry knowledge with technical expertise to create actionable, scalable, and business-aligned analytics solutions.
